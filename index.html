Introduction

Hello! My name is Daniel Park, and I am a currently a First-Year Student at the Franklin W. Olin College of Engineering! Although I'm not declared yet, I currently am planning on studying Engineering with a Concentration in Computing based on my past experience in programming in high school. I spent my high school years programming for other people through projects, most notably my school's iOS School App, teaching young students how to code through theCoderSchool Syosset, and becoming an engineering leader in my community leading a exponentially growing Robotics Club and founding my school's Student Government Technology Committee.

Now that I'm at college, I spend my time learning new languages and software to continue developing with human-centered intentions. I'm currently in our school's PiNT Club with an ambition for developing solutions that put humans first over all.

Outside my engineering activities, I am a very outgoing person, never shying away from an opportunity to express myself. I enjoy playing soccer, running track, even up to the point where I won All-Conference Honors! I also like performing on the piano and playing the cello, where I have earned All-State Honors.
